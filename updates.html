Freshman Year

We all got phones.

The group chat was created before the first day of school.

It felt efficient. No more guessing where anyone was. No more waiting.

If someone was bored, someone else responded instantly.

We were always talking.


October

If the chat went quiet, he revived it.

If someone didn’t respond, he double-texted.

It was funny at first. Then it wasn’t.


December

He said he just couldn’t sleep.

He was still online when the rest of us logged off.
