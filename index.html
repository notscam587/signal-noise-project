Our Website

We built this in 8th grade during computer class.

It wasn’t supposed to be anything serious. Just somewhere to put photos and jokes and things we didn’t want to forget.

Back then none of us had phones. We didn’t need them. If someone wasn’t at the park, we waited. If someone was late, we assumed they’d show up.

Everything happened in person. If we fought, it lasted an hour. If we laughed, it lasted the whole day.

We promised we would never drift.


The Plan

High school was coming. We said we’d stay close no matter what.

Phones were starting to become normal. Everyone said they would make things easier.

We believed that.

We thought constant connection meant permanent closeness.


Sam said we should keep updating this site. “Just in case,” he joked.
